How the Always-Running Auction Works

  • Add Players: Select free agents and add them to the auction with a starting bid
  • 24-Hour Rule: Once the first bid is placed, the player must stay in auction for at least 24 hours
  • Remove Players: After 24 hours have elapsed since the first bid, you can award the player to the highest bidder
  • No Bids: Players without any bids can be removed at any time
  • Winning: When you remove a player with bids, they are automatically awarded to the highest bidder with a 1-year contract
